How To Make Pilau

Pilau is a popular East African rice dish (especially Kenya &Tanzania) made by cooking rice with beef,chicken or vegetables in broth spices.

It mostly originated from Arabs& Indians.

    Ingridients
  • Long grain basmati rice
  • Ground pilau masala spices
  • Diced stewing beef
  • Crushed garlic &ginger
  • Bay leaves(optional)
  • Stock cubes(optional)
  • Whole spices(cumim seeds,black peppercons,cloves,cadamonpods&two cinamon sticks)
  • Cooking oil
  • Beef broth
  • Onions
  • Tomato paste
  • Salt to taste
    Method to prepare
  1. If not using stoe-bought seasoning, prepare the ground pilau masala spices by roasting the whole spices. Grind into powder.
  2. Rince the rice and soak with cold water.
  3. Soak the whole spices in a cup.
  4. Boil the meat with water,salt& the stock cube until tender. Seave the broth and set aside.
  5. Fry the meat and onions untill brown then add garlic,ginger and tomatopaste. Add the soaked whole spices and pilau masala powder.
  6. Next add the broth and water plus the rice
  7. Finally, cook until the rice is tender and all the liquid has everporated
  8. Serve with salad on the side
